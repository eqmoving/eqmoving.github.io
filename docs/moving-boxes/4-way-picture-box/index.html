4 Way Picture Box

Dimentions: 40" x 4" x 38"

Picture/Mirror box, packed in an outer wrap for shipping.

Product Description

Do you need to transport your art collection or a delicate antique mirror but the thought of breaking something gives you a headache? We can provide salvation! Our brand new Frame Moving Boxes are specifically designed to accommodate thin items such as paintings and mirrors, to make your life much easier when it comes to moving. These boxes are made of the highest quality material to guarantee optimum strength, lightness and comfort. Despite the fact that they are almost weightless, our Frame Moving Boxes comply with the industry’s standard of density and provide extra firm support in case of unwanted external pressure. Please remember to fold items into our bubble wrap and tape its corners before placing any items into the boxes; this will ensure maximum security.

The boxes are of universal dimension (40”x4”x38”) so you will have no problem fitting most items into a box. Frame Moving Boxes come in four bundle sizes – 8, 12, 16 or 20 items – so you have the freedom to choose the right one according to your needs. Keep in mind that you can normally fit two to three average-sized paintings into each box. Thinner items such as posters would need fewer boxes while a thick frame or a drawing might take up a whole box. You can measure the dimensions of your belongings to determine the number of Frame Moving Boxes you need and then you can select an appropriate bundle size to purchase. When packing, to avoid disorder and to save time, use our box marker to label each box with a description of its contents. Frame Moving Boxes are recyclable so you are contributing to the environment after you have settled in!
